Every pairing ships at AAA. Yellow is always paired with ink — never with paper, never with itself, never with a second accent.
✓ Do
  • Fill the one primary CTA per screen
  • Mark a live filing, a signed passport, a currently-selected row
  • Carry the smile inside the logomark
  • Use as ::selection background
✗ Don't
  • Use yellow for warning states — we reserve it for success + brand
  • Put white, light gray, or ink-56 text on yellow
  • Fill large decorative blocks — it loses its signal value
  • Pair with a second accent colour — the palette is three colours only
Why this yellow
  • Bright enough to signal on ink at any size
  • Warm enough to feel archival — ties to paper + hang-tag metaphor
  • Passes AAA with black — 15.9 : 1
  • A canonical yellow — holds identity in CMYK print and screen
