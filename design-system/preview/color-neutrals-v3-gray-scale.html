When to pick this
The team ships a lot of layered UI — modals over panels over dashboards — and the alpha system keeps producing surprises. Solid tokens make every surface composition a lookup instead of a math problem, and they survive PDF export, email clients, and high-DPI print faithfully.
Cost
Loses the philosophical tagline "no grays defined — every neutral is ink or paper + alpha". That's a real brand asset in your README. If it still reads as you, this trade isn't worth it.
