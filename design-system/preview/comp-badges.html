Structural tones — the five shapes of a badge
Default Muted Outline Solid Accent
Default = most-used, for filterable metadata. Muted = "this is information, not a call to act." Outline = destructive-safe alternative to solid. Solid = rare, used when the badge itself is the primary focal point of a row. Accent = reserved for "live" and "filing" — brand-coded status.
